About Us

Welcome to Universal Stationery, your trusted destination for all stationery and art supply needs in Pakistan. Since our very beginning, we have worked with a clear purpose: to provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable stationery to everyone — whether you are a student preparing for exams, a teacher shaping the future, an artist expressing creativity, or a professional building a career. We believe that stationery is not just about pens and paper; it is about enabling people to think, create, and succeed in their personal and professional lives. Every product we offer is selected to add real value to your learning and creative journey. With a passion for excellence and commitment to innovation, Universal Stationery continues to grow as a brand you can trust for quality, affordability, and inspiration.

Our Story

Universal Stationery began with a simple idea — to make quality stationery accessible for everyone, no matter their background or budget. Starting as a small local shop, we served our first customers with only a few products but with a big dream in our hearts. Over time, we realized that stationery is not just pens and paper; it is about enabling people to learn better, work smarter, and express creativity freely. With this belief, we expanded step by step, building partnerships with trusted brands like Dollar, Pilot, Pelikan, Staedtler, Faber-Castell, and Staples, and reaching out to schools, offices, and individuals across Pakistan. Today, Universal Stationery is not just a supplier of pens and notebooks — it is a name that represents reliability, innovation, and growth.
